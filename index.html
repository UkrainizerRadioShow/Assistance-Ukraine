Let’s Make Change Together
Our mission is to provide assistance for Ukrainian women in NL affected by the war, while raising awareness of Ukrainian heritage through fundraising and education.

TRANSPARENCY

We are guided by sincerity, integrity and financial accountability, fostering trust with beneficiaries and supporters, by upholding honesty and strong moral principles in our actions and decisions.

COLLABORATION

Working together from Ukrainian and Canadian sides as a united team, combining diverse skills and perspectives to achieve common goals.

  EMPATHY

Recognition and understanding the experiences and emotions of those who face challenging circumstances.

  RESPECT

Treating every individual, regardless of race, gender, sexual orientation or religion, with dignity and consideration.
